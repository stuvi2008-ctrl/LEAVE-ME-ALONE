LEAVE ME ALONE

A 3D Rage Platformer Experience

You're trapped in your dream world. The exit appears near... but is it real? Navigate 6 progressively intense levels where perspective manipulation and camera trickery blur the line between escape and entrapment. Play the prototype below and discover the truth.

What Makes It Unique

A rage-bait platformer with 6 levels of psychological gameplay, camera deception, and an unforgettable plot twist.

Rage Bait Gameplay

😱

Progressive Difficulty
